privacy policy

We do not collect information or profile our visitors in any way.

Apart from that, we use third party services that might profile users (even if we would like them not to). We would very much like to use better services that do not collect information about users but we do not have enough money to pay for them. If you want to help us migrate to more ethical platforms you can support us making a donation.


Services that we use:

  • Github Pages (hosting)
  • Adobe Fonts (fonts)
  • Google Analytics (statistics)
  • YouTube (embedded videos)


If you have any questions or doubts, or if you want to make a donation, you can contact us at ciao@vuoto.xyz.
